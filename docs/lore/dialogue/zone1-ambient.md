# Zone 1 Ambient & Overheard Dialogue

Snippets Steve overhears while walking through The Sanctuary. These play randomly when entering areas or passing NPCs. They build atmosphere, deliver jokes, and hint at the darker dynamics beneath the surface.

"I just really feel like the universe brought me here for a reason. The reason might be that I googled 'affordable retreat near me' but the universe works through Google too."

"Do you accept insurance? No? How about vibrational alignment as a form of payment? ...Also no?"

(Steve freezes.)

(Steve doesn't know what samadhi is but feels guilty anyway.)

"I charged my crystals under the full moon last night. They feel different. Specifically, they feel wet, because it rained. But ALSO energetically different."

"Thank you for your oxygen. Thank you for your shade. Thank you for your presence. You're the most consistent relationship I've ever had."

"I'm going to bury my intentions in this garden. Literally. I wrote them on paper and I'm going to bury them. The facilitator said the earth would 'digest them into reality.' The groundskeeper said if I dig up his lavender he'll bury ME."
