# Zone 1 Enemy Encounter Dialogue

Notes:
- [BATTLE START] = beginning of combat
- [ATTACK] = spoken during attack animation
- [HIT] = reaction to taking damage
- [LOW HP] = below 25% health
- [DEFEAT] = final words before dropping
- [FLEE] = if enemy runs away

---

## The Oversharer

**[BATTLE START]**
"Oh my god, I'm so glad you're here. I really need to process something. No, sit down. SIT DOWN. So basically my mother never validated my—"

**[ATTACK: Trauma Dump]**
"And that's when I realized my inner child was drowning in an ocean of abandonment, and I looked at my therapist — well, she's not technically a therapist, she's a somatic intuitive — and I said—"

**[ATTACK: "Can I Share Something?"]**
"Can I be really vulnerable right now? Is this a safe space? I'm going to assume it's a safe space."

**[ATTACK: Ugly Cry]**
(Wailing. Snot. The kind of crying that makes bystanders physically uncomfortable.)
"I'M JUST FEELING... SO... MUCH...!"

**[HIT]**
"That activation you just gave me? I actually NEEDED that. Thank you for triggering my healing."

**[LOW HP]**
"I think — I think I'm actually completing something right now. This is what my breathwork teacher calls a *somatic release*."

**[DEFEAT]**
"I feel so much lighter. Thank you for holding space for that. Same time tomorrow? No? Okay. I'll just... process alone. Like always. Like ALWAYS."
(drops: tear-soaked journal page)

## The Essential Oil Warrior

**[BATTLE START]**
"Hey! Hey, wait. I just — can I just — have you heard of doTERRA? No, don't walk away, it's not what you think. It's not a pyramid scheme, it's a *wellness ecosystem with a tiered distribution model*."

**[ATTACK: Anoint]**
"RECEIVE THIS BLESSING!" (hurls peppermint oil)
"That's hitting your third eye. Can you feel that? The burning means it's working."

**[ATTACK: MLM Pitch]**
"Okay so imagine this: you sell to five friends, and THEY sell to five friends, and — are you drawing a triangle? Stop drawing a triangle."

**[ATTACK: "It's Not a Pyramid"]**
"A PYRAMID has a POINT at the TOP. This has a DIAMOND shape. Completely different geometry. I should know, I invested $6,000."

**[HIT]**
"Ouch! I have an oil for that, actually. Frankincense and clove. $47 for a 5ml bottle. Or $39 if you join as a Wellness Advocate."

**[LOW HP]**
"My upline told me there would be resistance. She said the universe tests your commitment to abundance right before the breakthrough."

**[DEFEAT — normal]**
"I just... I just need one more recruit and I qualify for the leadership retreat in Sedona. ONE more. I've been saying that for eleven months."
(drops: tiny bottle of overpriced oil)

**[DEFEAT — if Steve uses Cold Read or Accurate Insult]**
(Steve looks at her for three seconds and says something very quiet.)
"You've spent $6,000 on inventory. You've sold $340. To your mother."
(The Essential Oil Warrior stares at him for a long time.)
"...My mother bought out of pity, didn't she."
(They walk away slowly. Somehow this feels worse than winning.)

## The Boundary Violator

**[BATTLE START]**
(approaches Steve with open arms)
"I just want to say — I see you. I REALLY see you. And I feel like what you need right now is—"
**Steve:** "Please don't—"
(hugs Steve)
"—connection."

**[ATTACK: Unsolicited Hug]**
"Bring it in, bring it in. Let yourself be HELD. You're safe. Shhhhhh."
(Steve's arms are pinned.)

**[ATTACK: "I'm Just Reflecting Back What I See"]**
"I'm not judging, I'm just *observing* — you carry a lot of tension in your jaw. That's usually unexpressed rage toward a parental figure. Is it your father? It's your father, isn't it."

**[ATTACK: Boundary Lecture]**
"Boundaries are sacred, Steve. SACRED. I would NEVER cross someone's boundary. What I did was I *intuited an invitation* from your energy body. Your words said no but your aura said yes."

**[HIT]**
"Ow! Okay! I'm noticing a LOT of aggression here and I'm choosing not to absorb it because I have very healthy boundaries."

**[LOW HP]**
"You know what? I think this interaction is bringing up some of MY stuff. I need to check in with myself. Actually no, I need to check in with YOU. How are you feeling about what just happened between us?"

**[DEFEAT]**
"I... I think I need to go process this. In my journal. While sitting very close to a stranger."
(drops: consent card)

## The Competitive Meditator

**[BATTLE START]**
(Sitting in perfect lotus position. Opens one eye.)
"Oh. You're here. I was just finishing my morning practice. Four hours today. I don't usually mention that. I just mentioned it for context. How long do *you* meditate?"
**Steve:** "I don't."
(The Competitive Meditator's eye twitches.)

**[ATTACK: Performative Stillness]**
(Closes eyes. Sits in profound silence. Radiates smugness at a frequency that causes physical discomfort.)
"..."
(You can't attack someone who's meditating. It's rude. Right? Is it rude? Steve doesn't know the rules here.)

**[ATTACK: Humble Brag Sutra]**
"I wasn't going to share this, but during my meditation this morning I experienced a complete dissolution of the ego-self boundary and merged with the cosmic void for what felt like an eternity but was actually forty-five minutes which I know because I don't use a timer but my body just *knows*. Anyway, how's your practice?"

**[ATTACK: Spiritual One-Upmanship]**
Steve: (anything)
"Oh, that's beautiful. Yeah, I remember when I was at that stage. Before my third awakening. The first two didn't really count, they were more like *openings*."

**[HIT]**
(Doesn't react for a long moment.)
"I noticed that. I chose to observe the sensation without attachment. It was actually a gift."
(Another pause.)
"It hurt quite a lot though."

**[LOW HP]**
"This is... this is exactly the kind of test my guru warned me about. He said, 'The deeper you go, the more the universe challenges you.' Or was that my chiropractor? One of them."

**[DEFEAT]**
"I'm going to meditate on this loss. I'll emerge stronger. More enlightened. More humble. The MOST humble. No one will be as humble as—"
(walks away still talking)
(drops: premium meditation cushion)

## The Yoga Snob

**[BATTLE START]**
(Holding a pose that should not be possible.)
"Oh. Hello. Sorry, I was just doing my *personal practice*. You wouldn't know the sequence — it's a very specific Ashtanga lineage. I trained directly under a student of a student of Pattabhi Jois. Well, I did a workshop with someone who did. Close enough."

**[ATTACK: Adjustment]**
"Your posture is *screaming* at me. Here, let me just—"
(Physically rearranges Steve's body into a position it doesn't want to be in.)
"—there. Doesn't that feel better?"
**Steve:** "No. Nothing about this feels better."
"That's your fascia releasing. Or tearing. Same energy."

**[ATTACK: "In Mysore We..."]**
"In MYSORE — that's in India, Steve, it's where yoga was invented — well, not invented, *remembered* — anyway, in Mysore we practiced at 4 AM and nobody complained. We didn't even have MATS. We used the FLOOR. The STONE floor. And we were GRATEFUL."

**[ATTACK: Advanced Pose Flex]**
(Transitions into a pose that defies anatomy.)
"This is Eka Pada Rajakapotasana variation C. I wouldn't expect you to — oh, you're not even going to try? That's — yeah, no, that's fine. That's valid."
(It is clear from their face that they do not find it valid.)

**[HIT]**
"Careful! This body is a TEMPLE. An extremely well-maintained, Mysore-trained temple."

**[LOW HP]**
"I think — I think my practice didn't prepare me for this. Unless — is this what Shavasana feels like from the inside? Am I dying? I'm dying. This is fine. Death is just the ultimate Shavasana."

**[DEFEAT]**
"I'll be in child's pose if anyone needs me. (collapses) This is restorative. I'm CHOOSING this."
(drops: overpriced yoga mat)

## The Karma Yogi (Broken Volunteer)

**[BATTLE START]**
(Approaches Steve with a mop and the expression of someone who has cleaned up after 200 consecutive breathwork sessions.)
"Hi! Welcome! Can I get you anything? Tea? A blanket? An emotional processing session? I'm available for all of it. I'm ALWAYS available. I haven't had a day off in four months! Isn't that... isn't that..."
(The smile flickers.)
"...wonderful?"

**[ATTACK: Guilt Trip]**
"Some of us are here in SERVICE, Steve. Some of us don't get to PAY for our healing. Some of us EARN it. With our HANDS. These hands, Steve." (shows blistered hands) "These hands have peeled ten thousand carrots for people who won't even look me in the eye at the buffet."

**[ATTACK: Martyr's Shield]**
"Hit me again. It's fine. I'm USED to it. I'll just add it to the pile. Right between 'missed my sister's wedding' and 'forgot what weekends are.' This is GROWTH."

**[ATTACK: Passive Aggressive Cleaning]**
(Begins mopping directly around Steve's feet with aggressive energy.)
"Don't mind me. I'm just cleaning. It's my CALLING. It's definitely my CALLING and not just a thing I do for eighteen hours a day because I can't leave. I'M FINE."

**[HIT]**
"Thank you. I deserved that. I probably did something in a past life. That's what the facilitator tells me whenever I'm in pain. 'Past life karma.' Very convenient explanation for present-day suffering."

**[LOW HP]**
(Sits down. The mop clatters to the floor.)
"I used to be a dental hygienist. I had an apartment. I had a cat. Now I sleep in a room with eleven people and the cat's with my ex and I don't even know what year it is."

**[STEVE CAN CHOOSE: Fight or Help]**

**[If Steve chooses HELP]**
**Steve:** "Hey. You don't have to do this. Any of this."
**Karma Yogi:** (a crack in the facade) "I know. I KNOW. But every time I say I want to leave, someone tells me I'm 'running from my shadow' and then three people surround me for a 'compassionate intervention' which is just peer pressure with eye contact."
**Steve:** "Do you want help getting out?"
**Karma Yogi:** "...my passport is in the office. They said they'd keep it 'safe.' It's been there for four months."
[UNLOCKS SIDE QUEST: Free Labor Isn't Seva]

**[If Steve chooses FIGHT — game makes you feel bad]**
(Steve defeats them. They crumple with the mop.)
"It's okay. I forgive you. I forgive everyone. I HAVE to forgive everyone or they add another week to my seva commitment."
(drops: tattered volunteer t-shirt)
(Steve's conscience stat takes a hit.)

## The Shadow Work Enthusiast

**[BATTLE START]**
(Dressed in black. Carrying a dog-eared copy of a book they haven't finished.)
"Interesting. You have a very activated nervous system. I can see that from here. Have you done any shadow work?"
**Steve:** "I don't know what that is."
"Then it's DEFINITELY running you. The shadow is everything you've repressed, denied, and projected. And I can see yours. It's... big. It's following you like a cloud. Don't look behind you."
(Steve looks behind him.)
"I SAID don't look."

**[ATTACK: "That's Your Shadow"]**
(Steve attacks.)
"Whoa. That aggression? That's not about me. That's your shadow. You're *projecting*. Everything you don't like about me is actually something you don't like about yourself. I'm basically a mirror."
(50% damage reflected.)
**Steve:** "What if I just don't like you?"
"Classic shadow response."

**[ATTACK: Armchair Analysis]**
"I notice you have a pattern of seeking external validation through *employment*. That's textbook avoidant attachment. Have you considered that your entire career is a coping mechanism for— "
**Steve:** "I'm an accountant because I'm good at math."
"Math is just structured anxiety. Look it up."

**[ATTACK: Integration Attempt]**
"I need you to just SIT with this feeling. Don't run from it. Don't analyze it. Just let it be here. Can you do that? Can you just... BE?"
**Steve:** "I've been 'being' my entire life. I've never stopped being. I'm being right now."
"Not like THAT."

**[HIT]**
"That's interesting. I'm going to sit with the pain of that. (pause) I've sat with it. My conclusion is that YOU have the problem."

**[LOW HP]**
"Okay. I'm going to be radically honest right now. I read the first three chapters of that Jung book. I've been improvising ever since. Nobody's noticed because everyone here is also improvising."

**[DEFEAT]**
"I think... I think my shadow is... that I use shadow work to avoid actually dealing with my own shit. That's... huh. That's actually a real insight. I don't like it. I don't like genuine insights at all."
(drops: dog-eared Jung book)
