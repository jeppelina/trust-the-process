# The Wife (Steve's Wife)

## Basic Info
- **Real Name:** [TBD — something normal like Karen or Linda]
- **Spiritual Name:** Changes every zone. By Burning Man she's going by something like "Luminara Starbloom"
- **Role:** The quest objective. Steve is chasing her through the spiritual pipeline.

## The Transformation Trail
Each zone you visit, people remember her — but she was different each time.

1. **Yoga Studio:** "Oh, she was lovely! Very focused on her alignment."
2. **Meditation Retreat:** "She had a powerful silence about her."
3. **Breathwork Workshop:** "She had the most beautiful release. She cried for three hours."
4. **Healing Center:** "She discovered her root chakra was completely blocked. We fixed that."
5. **Tantra Temple:** "She really opened up here." (Steve does not want details.)
6. **Ayahuasca Ceremony:** "She met her spirit animal. It told her to leave her old life behind."
7. **Men's Camp:** People mention a woman who "held space for the masculine" (unclear what this means)
8. **Poly House:** She briefly dated 4 people simultaneously and left them all amicably
9. **Festival Circuit:** Multiple sightings, always just missed her, always described differently
10. **Burning Man:** She's running a camp. She's thriving. She barely recognizes Steve.
