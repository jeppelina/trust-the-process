# Zone 1 Enemies: The Sanctuary

## Regular Enemies

### The Oversharer
**Description:** A retreat guest who corners you and won't stop telling you about their trauma. They didn't ask if you wanted to hear this. You didn't consent to this sharing.
**Appearance:** Wild eyes, tear-stained face, clutching a journal
**HP:** Low
**Attack pattern:**
- *Trauma Dump* — Unloads emotional baggage. Deals psychic damage. Steve loses HP from sheer discomfort.
- *"Can I Share Something?"* — It's not a question. Stuns Steve for one turn as he's too polite to say no.
- *Ugly Cry* — AOE attack. Everyone in range takes empathy damage.
**Weakness:** Boundaries. Steve's "I Have A Meeting" ability ends combat instantly.
**Drop:** Tear-soaked journal page (quest item / consumable)

---

### The Essential Oil Warrior
**Description:** Approaches you with a bottle of something that smells like a forest fire in a health food store. Wants to "support your immune system" and also recruit you into their downline.
**Appearance:** Lanyard with doTERRA badge, tote bag full of tiny bottles
**HP:** Medium
**Attack pattern:**
- *Anoint* — Throws essential oil. Deals poison damage + confusion (the smell is overwhelming).
- *MLM Pitch* — Multi-turn attack that gets stronger each round as they escalate from casual mention to hard sell.
- *"It's Not a Pyramid"* — Defense buff. They genuinely believe this.
**Weakness:** Psychic. Steve's verbal attacks (Insult, Guilt Trip, Cold Read) target her insecurity about the MLM. "Your ponytail is a cry for help" does bonus damage. At higher tiers, Accurate Insult reveals her actual income ($-2,400/year).
**Drop:** Tiny bottle of overpriced oil (consumable: minor heal + smells weird)

---

### The Boundary Violator (Disguised as Boundary Expert)
**Description:** Talks constantly about consent and boundaries while having none themselves. Touches you without asking "because they felt called to." Gives unsolicited advice framed as "reflections."
**Appearance:** Flowing clothes, lots of jewelry, maintains uncomfortable eye contact
**HP:** Medium
**Attack pattern:**
- *Unsolicited Hug* — Physical attack disguised as love. "You looked like you needed one."
- *"I'm Just Reflecting Back What I See"* — Psychic attack. Delivers a cutting personal observation with a loving smile.
- *Boundary Lecture* — Ironic buff: they become harder to hit while explaining why boundaries are sacred.
**Weakness:** Actually setting a boundary. Steve's "Per My Previous Email" ability creates an unbreakable wall.
**Drop:** Consent card (shield item — see items list)

---

### The Competitive Meditator
**Description:** Sits in the front row of every session. Meditates longer, breathes deeper, cries harder. Spirituality is their sport and they are winning.
**Appearance:** Full mala beads, meditation cushion they brought from home, slight smirk during other people's shares
**HP:** Medium-High
**Attack pattern:**
- *Performative Stillness* — Does nothing for 2 turns, but gains massive defense buff (you can't attack someone who's meditating, that's rude).
- *Humble Brag Sutra* — "I wasn't going to share this, but during my 4-hour meditation this morning..." Deals ego damage.
- *Spiritual One-Upmanship* — Counter-attack. Whatever Steve describes, they've done it deeper.
**Weakness:** Being ignored. Steve's "I Don't Care" passive aura slowly drains their HP.
**Drop:** Premium meditation cushion (equipment: +defense when sitting)

---

### The Yoga Snob
**Description:** Has opinions about your form, your mat, your clothes, your breathing, and your entire approach to existence. Trained in Mysore for 6 months and will never let you forget it.
**Appearance:** $200 yoga pants, Manduka mat, body that makes Steve feel terrible about himself
**HP:** High (very fit)
**Attack pattern:**
- *Adjustment* — Physically moves Steve into an uncomfortable position. Deals damage over time.
- *"In Mysore We..."* — Lecture attack. Lengthy. Unavoidable. Steve cannot leave without being rude.
- *Advanced Pose Flex* — Shows off an impossible pose. Deals intimidation damage.
**Weakness:** Steve's complete inability to do yoga means adjustments don't work properly. Immune to intimidation because he has zero ego invested in this.
**Drop:** Overpriced yoga mat (equipment: +agility, but Steve doesn't know how to use it)

---

### The Karma Yogi (Broken Volunteer)
**Description:** Been volunteering for 8 months. Hasn't been paid. Can't leave because that would mean they're "not surrendered." Eyes say "help me" while mouth says "I'm so grateful for this opportunity."
**Appearance:** Exhausted, wearing the same Sanctuary t-shirt they were given on day one, slightly manic smile
**HP:** Very low (they're exhausted)
**Attack pattern:**
- *Guilt Trip* — "Some of us are here in SERVICE" — deals guilt damage
- *Martyr's Shield* — Absorbs damage by suffering more. "It's fine. I'm fine. This is growth."
- *Passive Aggressive Cleaning* — Aggressively sweeps near Steve while sighing loudly
**Weakness:** Any kindness. If Steve uses a supportive ability, they break down crying and become an ally/info source instead of an enemy.
**Note:** These should be morally uncomfortable to fight. The game should make you feel bad. That's the point. Maybe Steve can choose to NOT fight them, and instead help them, which unlocks a side quest.
**Drop:** Tattered volunteer t-shirt (equipment: +defense, -dignity)

---

### The Shadow Work Enthusiast
**Description:** Has done enough therapy to be dangerous. Psychoanalyzes everyone around them using half-understood Jungian concepts. Everything is your "shadow." Especially if you disagree with them.
**Appearance:** Intense, possibly wearing all black "to integrate their shadow," carries a copy of a book they haven't finished
**HP:** Medium
**Attack pattern:**
- *"That's Your Shadow"* — Any attack Steve makes is reflected back as "projection." 50% damage reflection.
- *Armchair Analysis* — Unprovoked psychological reading. "I notice you have a pattern of..." Deals confusion damage.
- *Integration Attempt* — Tries to force Steve to "sit with" an uncomfortable feeling. Stun for 1-2 turns.
**Weakness:** Steve's "Cold Read" ability — seeing through their half-understood Jungian persona to the actual person underneath deals massive damage.
**Drop:** Dog-eared Jung book (consumable: temporary intelligence buff, but Steve doesn't understand most of it)

## Elite Enemies (Mini-bosses / Rare Encounters)

### The Facilitator Diva
**Description:** Runs the most popular workshop at The Sanctuary. Has a waiting list. Treats Karma Yogis like personal servants. Cries about "the pain of holding so much space for others" while doing the least actual work of anyone here.
**Appearance:** Ethereal flowing outfit, flower crown, always backlit somehow
**HP:** High
**Attack pattern:**
- *Emotional Labor Claim* — "Do you have any idea how much I give?" Defense + attack buff.
- *Workshop Recruitment* — Tries to get Steve into her $500 "intensive." If successful, drains gold.
- *Vulnerability Performance* — Cries on command. Stuns all attackers. "How dare you come at me when I'm processing?"
- *Flying Monkeys* — Summons 2 loyal followers to defend her.
**Weakness:** Steve pointing out she hasn't actually done anything today. Reality damage is super effective.
**Drop:** Flower crown (equipment: +charisma, +hypocrisy)

### The Gatekeeper
**Description:** An elder/long-timer who controls access to the advanced areas. Decides who is "ready" based on entirely subjective vibes. Uses spiritual language to maintain power. You cannot get to the boss without getting past this person.
**Appearance:** Silver hair, prayer beads, knowing smile that says "I see through you (and I enjoy the power this gives me)"
**HP:** Very high
**Attack pattern:**
- *"You're Not Ready"* — Blocks access. Cannot be bypassed through normal combat.
- *Spiritual Gaslighting* — "If you're frustrated, that's the work." Turns Steve's attacks against him.
- *Ancient Wisdom* — Quotes something vaguely Eastern. Gains authority buff from nearby NPCs who nod along.
- *Conditional Approval* — Offers to let Steve through IF he does something humiliating first.
**Weakness:** Steve's "Cold Read" ability reveals the Gatekeeper has only been here 18 months and just talks with confidence.
**Drop:** Access key to ceremony space (quest item)

## NPC (Non-Combat Characters)

### Local Groundskeeper
- From the nearby town. Works for minimum wage. Sees everything.
- Delivers dry, honest commentary on what's really going on.
- Source of side quests and practical help (knows where things actually are, unlike the spiritual people who give directions like "follow your heart to the east").
- Possibly becomes a recurring ally across zones.

### The Newcomer
- Another first-timer who arrived the same day as Steve.
- Gradually gets pulled in over the course of the zone.
- Serves as a warning/mirror: this is what happens if Steve drinks the Kool-Aid.
- Possible future companion?

### The Secretly Skeptical Karma Yogi
- Whispers real information when the facilitators aren't looking.
- Knows about Steve's wife. Will share info in exchange for help.
- Trapped here by social pressure, not belief.

### The Celebrity Guest
- A minor influencer or micro-celebrity doing a "digital detox" (they're still posting).
- Gets special treatment that breaks all the supposed rules about equality.
- Everyone pretends not to notice.
