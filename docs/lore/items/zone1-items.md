# Zone 1 Items: The Sanctuary

## Equipment

### Weapons (Things Steve Can Wield)

**The Orientation Pamphlet** (Starting item / Evidence)
- A tri-fold brochure full of buzzwords and stock photos of people meditating at sunset
- Stats: Evidence item (exposes pricing structure when used in dialogue)
- Flavor text: *"Welcome to The Sanctuary of Infinite Becoming! Please note the non-refundable deposit policy on page 3 (in very small print)."*

**Sage Bundle** (Throwable / Grenade equivalent)
- AOE smoke damage + confusion to enemies
- Stats: Deals 15 damage in radius, applies "Smudged" debuff (accuracy -20% for 3 turns)
- Flavor text: *"Supposedly clears negative energy. Definitely clears your lungs."*

**Singing Bowl** (Blunt weapon)
- A brass Tibetan singing bowl. Heavy enough to hurt.
- Stats: +8 attack, chance to apply "Resonating" stun
- Flavor text: *"Hand-forged by monks. Or mass-produced in a factory in Guangzhou. Nobody can tell the difference and that's beautiful."*

**The Really Big Crystal** (Two-handed weapon)
- An amethyst cluster the size of a football
- Stats: +15 attack, -5 speed (it's heavy)
- Flavor text: *"Retail value: $800. Combat value: surprisingly high. Spiritual value: according to the seller, infinite."*

### Armor / Wearables

**Steve's Polo Shirt** (Starting armor)
- His tucked-in polo from the office. Out of place everywhere.
- Stats: +2 defense, +10 Normality aura (enemies find him confusing)
- Flavor text: *"100% polyester. Machine washable. Spiritually impenetrable."*

**Tattered Volunteer T-Shirt** (Drop from Karma Yogi)
- The Sanctuary branded. Stained with kitchen grease and tears.
- Stats: +5 defense, -3 dignity, +5 empathy
- Flavor text: *"I volunteered 600 hours at The Sanctuary and all I got was this t-shirt. Literally. That's all I got."*

**Flower Crown** (Drop from Facilitator Diva)
- Handmade. Instagram-ready. Smells like wilting flowers.
- Stats: +8 charisma, -5 credibility with normal people
- Flavor text: *"Wearing this grants you the power to say 'I feel called to...' before making any request and have it taken seriously."*

**Overpriced Yoga Pants** (Shop item)
- Lululemon or equivalent. Steve doesn't understand the pricing.
- Stats: +5 agility, +3 flexibility
- Flavor text: *"$128. For pants. Steve's entire work wardrobe cost less than this single garment."*

**Mala Bead Necklace** (Found item)
- 108 beads. Each one charged with "intention" (static electricity).
- Stats: +3 defense, +2 insight gain per battle
- Flavor text: *"You're supposed to use these for counting mantras. Steve uses them for counting the minutes until this retreat is over."*

### Accessories

**Consent Card** (Drop from Boundary Violator)
- A laminated card explaining your boundaries. Works as a shield.
- Stats: Blocks one physical attack per battle (the hug, the unsolicited touch)
- Flavor text: *"GREEN means I'm open to connection. YELLOW means ask first. RED means Steve's current permanent state."*

**Nametag (Spiritual Name)** (Given by NPC)
- The Sanctuary assigns you a spiritual name at check-in.
- Stats: +5 Insight gain, NPCs react differently to you
- Flavor text: *"Steve has been renamed 'Akash Prem' which means 'sky love.' He will not be using this."*
- (Fun mechanic: if Steve actually uses the spiritual name, certain NPCs become friendly but Steve's Normality stat drops)

**The Wi-Fi Password** (Rare quest reward)
- Written on a scrap of paper. The Founder's cabin has WiFi. Nobody else does.
- Stats: Allows Steve to access "the outside world" for specific quest actions
- Flavor text: *"The password is 'consciousness2012'. Of course it is."*

## Consumables

**Cacao Ceremony Drink** (Healing item)
- A cup of ceremonial-grade cacao. Tastes like someone melted a candle into hot water.
- Effect: Restore 30 HP, +2 temporary charisma
- Flavor text: *"Heart-opening properties not scientifically verified. Does contain caffeine though, so Steve's grateful."*

**Kombucha (Homemade)** (Healing item)
- Brewed in the kitchen by a Karma Yogi who "just felt called to ferment."
- Effect: Restore 15 HP, 10% chance of food poisoning (lose 5 HP)
- Flavor text: *"Contains live cultures and possibly also something that shouldn't be alive."*

**Mystery Supplement** (Buff item)
- Unmarked capsules someone handed Steve during a workshop. "Just take these, trust me."
- Effect: Random buff OR random debuff. You don't know until you take it.
- Flavor text: *"Steve is not going to take random pills from a stranger. ...Right?"*

**Essential Oil Blend** (Thrown consumable)
- An aggressive peppermint-eucalyptus-frankincense combo.
- Effect: Deals 10 damage to one enemy (olfactory assault), minor heal to Steve (placebo)
- Flavor text: *"'Thieves Blend' — named either after the historical plague remedy or the business model."*

**The Kitchen's Best Attempt at Coffee** (Buff item)
- It's chicory root and dandelion. It is not coffee.
- Effect: +1 energy, -10 hope
- Flavor text: *"This is not coffee. Steve knows it's not coffee. The retreat knows it's not coffee. But everyone has agreed to call it coffee."*

**Actual Coffee** (Rare consumable)
- Smuggled in by the Local Groundskeeper. Contraband.
- Effect: Full HP restore, +20% damage for 3 turns, Steve feels human again
- Flavor text: *"Black. No milk, no sugar, no ceremony. Just coffee. Steve almost cries."*

## Quest Items

**Wife's Credit Card Receipt** (Starting quest item)
- The charge that led Steve here. $2,400 for a "Transformational Immersion."
- *"Line item: 1x Transformational Immersion (non-refundable). Steve has thoughts about the non-refundable part."*

**Recommendation Letter from The Founder** (Boss drop / quest completion)
- A handwritten letter on handmade paper introducing the bearer to [Zone 2].
- *"To whom it may concern (though we don't use that word here — try 'to whom it may resonate')..."*

**Karma Yogi Work Schedule** (Evidence item)
- A spreadsheet showing volunteer hours. Steve is appalled at the labor violations.
- *"40-hour work weeks with no pay. Steve has seen this before. It was in an OSHA complaint."*

**The Founder's Financial Records** (Evidence item for boss fight)
- Retrieved from the Back Office. Reveals the true economics of The Sanctuary.
- *"Revenue: $1.2M/year. Volunteer labor savings: $400K. Founder's 'modest stipend': $180K. Tax status: non-profit. Steve's eye twitches."*
